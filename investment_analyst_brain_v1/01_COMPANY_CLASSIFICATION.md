# Company Classification Policy v1.0

No valuation starts before classification.

Classes:
- High Growth — Profitable: use DCF, EV/EBITDA, P/E, PEG, Price/FCF.
- High Growth — Transition to Profitability: use Forward EV/Sales, EV/Sales, low-weight scenario DCF, Morningstar, analyst consensus. Avoid P/E.
- Mature Cash Generator: use DCF, P/E, Price/FCF, EV/EBITDA, DDM when suitable.
- Cyclical: use normalized mid-cycle earnings, EV/EBITDA, EV/Sales, normalized DCF.
- Capital Intensive: focus on CapEx, debt, EV/EBITDA, explicit-CapEx DCF.
- Financial Institution: use P/B, ROE, Residual Income, DDM.
- REIT: use AFFO, NAV, dividend yield, cap rate.
- Early Stage / Pre-Profit: use EV/Sales, TAM evidence, runway, dilution, milestones.
- Commodity: use normalized commodity prices and mid-cycle EBITDA/FCF.
- Holding Company: use Sum of the Parts.

Required output:
classification, evidence, suitable models, excluded models, confidence.
