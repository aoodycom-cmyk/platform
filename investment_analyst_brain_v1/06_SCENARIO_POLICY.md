# Scenario Policy v1.0

Required scenarios:
- Conservative
- Base
- Optimistic
- Exceptional, only when plausible

Each scenario must include:
revenue path, margins, FCF, CapEx, WACC, terminal growth or exit multiple, fair value, probability, required conditions, main risk.

Conservative + Base + Optimistic probabilities must total 100%.
Exceptional stays separate unless explicitly included.
Every price must answer: what must the company achieve to justify it?
