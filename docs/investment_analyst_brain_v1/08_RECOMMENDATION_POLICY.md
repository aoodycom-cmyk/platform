# Recommendation Policy v1.0

Inputs:
margin of safety, business quality, forecast confidence, data quality, risk, scenario asymmetry, dilution, balance sheet.

BUY:
adequate margin of safety and acceptable risk/reward.

HOLD:
fairly valued, weak margin of safety, or meaningful uncertainty.

SELL / AVOID:
material downside, thesis deterioration, unacceptable financing/dilution risk, or valuation requiring an exceptional scenario.

Every decision must state:
why, why not, biggest assumption, what changes the decision, confidence.

If critical data is missing, output INSUFFICIENT DATA.
