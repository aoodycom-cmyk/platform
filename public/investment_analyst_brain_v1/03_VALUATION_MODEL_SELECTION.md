# Valuation Model Selection Policy v1.0

Use at least two suitable models when possible.

- DCF: only when cash generation can be forecast credibly.
- Reverse DCF: diagnostic only.
- P/E: only with positive normalized EPS.
- PEG: supporting check for profitable growth.
- EV/EBITDA: for operating comparisons.
- EV/Sales: for early-stage or transition companies.
- Price/FCF: for positive-FCF companies.
- Historical multiples: only when the business regime is comparable.
- Peer multiples: only with genuine peers.
- Morningstar and analyst consensus: external references only.

No model may exceed 45% without explicit justification.
External references combined should normally not exceed 25%.
