# Forecast Policy v1.0

Source priority:
1. Company guidance
2. Analyst estimates
3. Historical trends
4. Backlog, contracts, unit economics
5. Sector assumptions
6. Clearly labeled methodology defaults

Forecast yearly, not with one unexplained constant rate.

FCF bridge:
Revenue → Operating income → Tax/NOPAT → D&A → CapEx → Working capital → FCF

CapEx priority:
guidance → commentary → historical CapEx/revenue → investment cycle → sector default.

Use diluted shares and model dilution when material.
