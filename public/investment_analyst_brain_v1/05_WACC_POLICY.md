# WACC Policy v1.0

Explain:
risk-free rate, equity risk premium, beta/business-risk proxy, cost of equity, cost of debt, tax rate, capital weights, final WACC, sensitivity.

Raise WACC for:
pre-profit status, concentration, cyclicality, financing risk, small scale, regulation.

Lower WACC for:
recurring revenue, net cash, strong moat, diversification, predictable FCF.

Guardrails:
- Mature high-quality: 7.5%–9.5%
- Profitable growth: 8.5%–10.5%
- Transition to profitability: 10%–13%
- Early-stage/high uncertainty: 12%–16%
- Cyclical: 9.5%–13%

Always show at least ±1% sensitivity.
