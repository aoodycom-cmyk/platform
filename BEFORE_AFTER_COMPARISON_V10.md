## Main Changes

| Area | Before | After |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Home | Functional but still dashboard-like | Branded product entry with one question, search, quick actions, recent analyses, and watchlist |
| Report header | Company information was present but less decision-led | Current Price, Fair Value, Recommendation, and Confidence appear immediately |
| Decision card | Large but more calculator-like | More premium hierarchy with clearer key metrics |
| Mobile rhythm | Usable but dense | iPhone-first spacing and no horizontal overflow |
| Product identity | Generic platform naming | Franklin Research V10 identity in app, browser title, and PWA metadata |
